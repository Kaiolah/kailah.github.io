I remember alsways being cautious about asking her to buy certain things because I knew she'd go out of the way, bending over backwards to get it done for me, even if that meants that she would be missing out on something for herself. It was so enjoyable to see her smiling as she tried on outfits. although I couldn't be with her physically, I felt so bonded and it was turly so much fun. Being able to treat my mom after years of her treating me touched me very deeply.
