About

Community is a show about 7 unlikely comrades that come together for a spanish study group. When hotshot lawyer Jeff Winger has is liscense suspended becuase his college degree was fake, he enrolls in commuity college. In order to impress a girl he's attracted to, he invents a fake spanish 101 study group that turns into something way bigger.

The setting is a community college based on the actual Glendale Community College that the show creator Dan Harmon attended. The flag is a butthole which I think is hilarious.
